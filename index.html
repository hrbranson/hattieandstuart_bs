Welcome to the website for Hattie and Stuart's wedding!

Here you will find all the information you need about the day, including accommodation, transportation and registry details. Please do contact us at hattieandstuart@gmail.com if you have any further questions. We look forward to seeing you on the day!

Saturday 22nd March 2025

St. Andrew's Church, Prestwold

Leicestershire, LE12 5SH

RSVPs are now closed!

What should I wear?

The dress code is formal. Ladies, please do not wear white or shades of white. As the walk from St. Andrew's Church to Prestwold Hall is gravel, we recommend a block heel rather than a stiletto. Gentlemen must wear a suit with tie. Military guests please refrain from wearing uniform or medals; you are welcome to wear a military tie with your suit. People not dressed appropriately will not be permitted into the ceremony.


What time should I arrive?

The ceremony begins at 1:30 pm, so we ask that you arrive by 1 pm to get seated.


Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will take place in St. Andrew's Church on the Prestwold estate, and the heating will be on. The church is a short 5-minute walk from Prestwold Hall, across a gravel path. The remainder of the day will be inside Prestwold Hall with gardens available to get some fresh air if needed.


Can I take pictures with my phone or camera during the wedding?

We ask that you do not take any photos or videos during the ceremony as we want you all to be present with us in the moment. We have two professional photographers to capture everything, and we will share these with you after the wedding. After the ceremony, yes! Please feel free to take as many photos or videos as you like. Don't forget to share them with us on WhatsApp or email them to us at hattieandstuart@gmail.com.


Can I bring a plus one?

We have limited availability for additional guests. If you would like to bring a plus one, please send us a message and we will let you know.


Is there parking?

Car parking is available at Prestwold Hall and St. Andrew's Church. You are able to leave your car overnight in the car park as long as they are collected by 10 am Sunday 23rd March.

Prestwold Hall is in a rural location. We strongly advise you pre-book a taxi (or Uber) for midnight when the party finishes. Loughborough is a 10-minute drive from Prestwold Hall. There is a direct train from Loughborough to London St. Pancras International, but check the last train time in advance.
